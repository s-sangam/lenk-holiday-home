About the Property

This charming chalet is located at the end of a quiet footpath in Lenk im Simmental, surrounded by tranquil nature and offering panoramic views of the Wildstrubel massif. A sunny south-facing terrace and garden make it an ideal retreat for nature lovers.

The chalet features a bright living and dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and three bedrooms with high ceilings. The bathroom offers both shower and bathtub. Downstairs, you’ll find a laundry and storage room. Free outdoor parking is available.

Whether you’re coming for skiing, hiking, or simply to relax, Chalet Strubel offers cozy comfort and an authentic Swiss mountain experience.
